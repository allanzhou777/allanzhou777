Do Re Me Fa So La Ti Do

In December of 2019, I did my first street performance and earned almost $100. This started my public performance journey, where I played in restaurants, nursery homes, libraries, fundraisers, you name it! In the summer of 2021, I combined efforts with three other cellists, forming the AMY Cello Quartet, named after our cello lesson teacher.

As our name and popularity grew, we got more and more opportunities. On one occasion, we played for Gov. Greg Abbott in the Texas State Capitol, and a few months later. Then, in 2022, I played in the Westlake Nutcracker Spectacular, performing in the Finale for over 6,000 attendees.
